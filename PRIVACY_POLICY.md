# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Effective Date / 生效日期:** 2026-02-25

This Privacy Policy applies to md2jpeg (the "App").  
本隐私政策适用于 md2jpeg（以下简称“本应用”）。

## 1. Information We Collect / 我们收集的信息

The App does not collect, store, or upload your personal information.  
本应用不会收集、存储或上传你的个人信息。

Any Markdown content you type or paste is processed locally on your device.  
你输入或粘贴的 Markdown 内容仅在你的设备本地处理。

## 2. Permission Usage / 权限使用说明

The App requests Photos permission when you export and save images.  
本应用会在你导出并保存图片时请求“照片”权限。

This permission is used only to write generated images to your system photo library.  
该权限仅用于将你生成的图片写入系统照片库。

The App does not read, analyze, or upload your existing photo library content.  
本应用不会读取、分析或上传你的现有相册内容。

## 3. How Data Is Processed / 数据处理方式

- Markdown rendering is performed locally.  
  Markdown 渲染在本地完成
- Image export (PNG/JPEG/HEIC) is performed locally.  
  图片导出（PNG/JPEG/HEIC）在本地完成
- The App has no account system and no cloud sync.  
  本应用不提供账号系统，也不进行云端同步

## 4. Third-Party Services / 第三方服务

The App does not include ad SDKs and does not use third-party analytics or tracking services.  
本应用不集成广告 SDK，不使用第三方分析/埋点服务。

When rendering Mermaid diagrams, the App uses bundled frontend resources for local display only.  
本应用在渲染 Mermaid 图表时使用内置前端资源，仅用于本地显示。

## 5. Data Security / 数据安全

Because the App does not send your content to remote servers, primary data risk is limited to your local device environment.  
由于本应用不将你的内容发送到服务器，主要数据风险来自设备本地环境。

We recommend using device-level protections (such as passcode/biometric lock and system updates).  
建议你使用设备系统提供的安全措施（如设备锁、系统更新）保护数据。

## 6. Children's Privacy / 儿童隐私

The App is not designed to collect personal information from children and does not actively collect personal data from any users.  
本应用不面向儿童定向收集个人信息，也不会主动收集任何个人信息。

## 7. Changes to This Policy / 隐私政策更新

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted in this file with a revised effective date.  
我们可能会不定期更新本隐私政策。更新后将通过本文件发布，并更新生效日期。

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the maintainer via the project repository:  
如果你对本隐私政策有任何问题，可通过项目仓库联系维护者：

`https://github.com/shengyang998/md2jpeg`
